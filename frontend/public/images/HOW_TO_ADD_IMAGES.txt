========================================
HOW TO ADD YOUR IMAGES
========================================

STEP 1: Find This Folder
-------------------------
You are here: frontend/public/images/

STEP 2: Copy Your Images Here
------------------------------
Drag and drop your images into this folder
OR
Right-click → Copy → Paste your images here

STEP 3: Rename Your Images
---------------------------
Required:
- logo.jpg (or logo.png) - Your business logo
- shop-front.jpg - Your shop photo

Optional:
- gallery-1.jpg - Gallery photo 1
- gallery-2.jpg - Gallery photo 2
- gallery-3.jpg - Gallery photo 3
- gallery-4.jpg - Gallery photo 4
- gallery-5.jpg - Gallery photo 5

STEP 4: Refresh Browser
------------------------
Press: Ctrl+R or F5

Done! Your images will appear on the website!

========================================
TIPS
========================================

✓ Use lowercase file names (logo.jpg not Logo.JPG)
✓ Use hyphens not spaces (shop-front.jpg not shop front.jpg)
✓ Supported formats: .jpg, .png, .webp, .gif
✓ Recommended sizes:
  - Logo: 200x200px
  - Shop Front: 1920x1080px
  - Gallery: 800x800px

========================================
EXAMPLE
========================================

Your folder should look like:

frontend/public/images/
├── logo.jpg          ← Your logo
├── shop-front.jpg    ← Your shop photo
├── gallery-1.jpg     ← Gallery photo 1
├── gallery-2.jpg     ← Gallery photo 2
└── gallery-3.jpg     ← Gallery photo 3
